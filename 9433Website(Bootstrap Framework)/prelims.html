World Wide Web

History of the World Wide Web. Difference of the World Wide Web and the internet. Hypertext Transfer Protocol, the protocol used by the world wide web

History

The World Wide Web is invented primarily on information sharing between scientists in the 1989 by Sir Tim Berners Lee and considered as the father of the World Wide Web .The World Wide Web's three fundamental technologies, the HyperText Markup Language(http), the Uniform Resource Identifier (URI) and the Hypertext Transfer Protocol.The WWW is a large web resource where people can access through internet and is identified by a URL(Uniform Resource Locator) e.g http://www.microsoft.com.

World Wide Web vs Internet

Internet is the interconnection of computers globally that makes them communicate. In the internet, information are able to travel through protocols - rule or the agreed format that is used to transmit data.
