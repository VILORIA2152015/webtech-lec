HTML (Hypertext Markup Language)

History

HTML was invented by Tim Berners Lee(considered as the father of the World Wide Web) in the year of 1989, the same year the HTTP and URI (Uniform Resource Identifier) was invented. HTML is one of the three fundamental technology of the World Wide Web. These technologies were invented for the purpose of sharing research purposes, HTTP being the protocol, URI for naming and HTML for displaying the output
